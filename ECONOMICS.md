# Economics & Unit Math

## The Value of a Converted Lead
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits (e.g., 30% off Anthropic or Cursor).
Assuming a target Series A/B startup spends ~$2,000/mo on AI tools, a 30% discount saves them $600/mo.
If Credex takes a 10% cut of total spend (or 33% of the savings), Credex earns ~$200/mo ($2,400/yr) per converted company.
**Estimated LTV**: Assuming a 2-year lifespan, LTV is **$4,800**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
With our zero-paid-budget GTM strategy:
- **Content / SEO (Blog Post)**: $0 marginal cost. Time cost: ~5 hours to write.
- **Community Injection (Slack/Reddit)**: $0 marginal cost. Time cost: ~1 hour.
- **Direct Outbound (LinkedIn)**: $0 marginal cost (using free LinkedIn limits).
- Let's assume we spend 10 hours a week on distribution. At an imputed founder rate of $100/hr, that's $1,000/week. 
- If this yields 5 qualified consultations and 1 closed deal per week, the **Blended CAC is $1,000**.

## The Conversion Funnel
To make this profitable and predictable:
1. **Audits Completed**: 100
2. **Emails Captured (View Report)**: 20 (20% conversion)
3. **Consultations Booked (High Savings)**: 4 (20% of emails captured)
4. **Credits Purchased (Closed Won)**: 1 (25% close rate on calls)

*100 audits -> 1 new customer.* 
If LTV is $4,800 and CAC is $1,000, the **LTV:CAC ratio is 4.8:1**, which is very healthy.

## The Path to $1M ARR
To reach $1,000,000 in ARR, Credex needs **~416 active customers** paying $2,400/yr.
At our funnel conversion rate (1%), we need **41,600 completed audits**.
Over 18 months, that's ~2,300 audits per month (~76/day).
**What must be true:** 
To hit 76 audits/day organically, the tool must have a viral loop. The "Shareable Public URL" (showing "I saved $500/mo using this tool") needs a K-factor > 0.2. Every 10 audits shared must bring in 2 new users. We also need to embed the calculator directly into partner newsletters and VC "perk" portals to guarantee distribution.
