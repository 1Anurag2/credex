A: No, the audit is completely free. We just want to help startups optimize their runway.

**Q: Do I need to connect my bank or Ramp account?**
A: Nope. You just manually select the tools your team uses and input the approximate number of seats or monthly spend.

**Q: How do you calculate savings?**
A: We maintain a real-time database of pricing tiers for Cursor, Claude, OpenAI, GitHub Copilot, and more. Our engine calculates if a cheaper tier, team plan, or alternative tool fits your team's specific use case better.

**Q: Why do you ask for my email at the end?**
A: We only ask for an email if you want a detailed PDF report sent to you, or if our engine finds significant savings that require a brief consultation to unlock (via discounted credits).

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex is a marketplace for discounted AI infrastructure credits. We help startups buy credits for tools like Cursor and Claude at up to 30% off retail pricing.
